---
name: Second Maroon War
era: early-republic-and-loyalism
start_date: 1795-01-01
end_date: 1796-01-01
notes: > 
  Jamaican Maroons, descendants of escaped slaves living in the mountainous
  interior of the colony, attack the British after a series of public
  humiliations. The eight-month war ended in Maroon defeat and a deportation of
  the Maroon population to Nova Scotia.
