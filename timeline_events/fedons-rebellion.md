start_date: 1795-03-02

notes: Inspired by the Haitian Revolution, a group of mixed-race free people
  under the leadership of Julien Fédon and supported by some enslaved people rose
  up against the British in Grenada. The revolt was brutally repressed and
  ultimately failed.
