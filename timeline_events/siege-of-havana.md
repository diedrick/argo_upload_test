---
name: Siege of Havana
era: seven-years-war
start_date: 1762-06-06
end_date: 1762-08-13
notes: Following Spain's entry into the Seven Years' War on the side of the
  French in January 1762, the British attacked the crucial Spanish colony of Cuba.
  The British besieged the capitol of Havana for months, finally taking the city
  in August. The city remained under British occupation until it was returned to
  the Spanish the next year.
