---
name: Whiskey Rebellion
era: early-republic-and-loyalism
start_date: 1794-01-01
notes: > 
  Resistence to the 1791 Whisky Act culminates in an armed insurrection in western
  Pennsylvania. As with the earlier Shays' Rebellion, the Whiskey Rebellion
  represented a major challenge to the new United States government and the
  balance of power between the federal and state governments.
