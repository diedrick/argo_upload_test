---
name: First Carib War 
era: imperial-crisis
start_date: 1769-01-01
end_date: 1773-01-01
notes: > 
  Alarmed by British encroachments into their lands on St Vincent—ceded to the
  British by the French following the 1763 Treaty of Paris—Carib people take up
  arms. The war ended in a stalemate and the creation of borders between British
  and Carib parts of the island.
---
