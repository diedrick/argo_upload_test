---
name: Tacky's Revolt
era: seven-years-war
start_date: 1760-04-07
end_date: 1761-01-01
notes: > 
  Amidst the chaos of the continuing Seven Years' War, a massive slave uprising
  led by Fante leader Tacky broke out across Jamaica. Although ultimately
  repressed, the revolt was the largest slave uprising in the Caribbean prior to
  the Haitian Revolution.
